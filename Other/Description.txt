These mods are not only a modification of the map indeed, I had different desires in the development of these mods:
[list]
[*]Instead of making a map over a specific year, I preferred to take a period of 10 to 25 years and present the countries, colonies, protectora, subjects, etc. that existed over the period.
[*]All mods aim to be very detailed (ex: Confederation of the Rhine or Small colonies like Montserrat, barbados, …).
[/list]
